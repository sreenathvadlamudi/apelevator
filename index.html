Frequently asked questions

What types of elevators do you install?
Passenger, freight, hospital, home elevators, and capsule lifts. We tailor to your shaft dimensions and load requirements.
Do you offer annual maintenance contracts?
Yes, flexible AMC plans with 24/7 emergency call support. Includes regular lubrication, safety checks, and priority service.
What areas do you serve?
Chittoor, Tirupati, and entire Chittoor district. Also parts of Andhra Pradesh border – just call us.
How long does installation take?
Typically 4-6 weeks depending on shaft readiness and customization. We work fast without compromising safety.
Are your elevators ISI certified?
All components meet BIS/ISO standards. We also offer fire-resistant doors and emergency battery lowering.
What is the cost of a home elevator?
It varies by travel, capacity, and finishes. We provide free site inspection and detailed quote within 24h.
